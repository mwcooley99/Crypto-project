What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a virtual or digital money built with cryptographic protocols which takes the form of tokens or "coins". It makes transactions secure and difficult to fake.

What is The Most Important Feature of Cryptocurrency?

It is not controlled by any central authority. In other words, it doesn't be manipulated and controlled by government .
